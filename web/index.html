Shrinked was created by two tech enthusiasts, Ivan & Artyom, who wanted to rediscover the magic in spoken stories.

This project transforms spoken words into structured data. It's a new way to know who said what — unlike vague AI summaries, with privacy and attribution at its core.

Try it with your favorite podcast or video

Generic AI Summaries

You're left with a condensed but often context-lacking summary that misses crucial details and nuances

  • Shallow summaries lacking depth
  • No sources references and timestamps
  • Not good for RAG - low information density
  • Limited to text, struggle with audio/video
  • Privacy concerns with data storage and usage

Advantages of structured documents

Shrinked transforms spoken word into comprehensive, structured documents, offering a context-preserving, easily navigable output

  • Original paraphrased format
  • Hierarchical auto-generated chapters and sections
  • RAG-optimized markup with timestamped references
  • Privacy-first approach - no content storage
  • Unlimited length audio/video input (in pro version)
